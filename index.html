HOW WE TRIAGE YOUR CALL

When you call us, our receptionists use SMARTnavigation

A structured triage system designed by GPs that helps our reception team direct you to the right care, first time. It's not AI - it's clinically guided scripts aligned with NHS guidance.

This means you get seen by the right person, faster.

1

You call the surgery

Our receptionist will ask about your symptoms

2

Guided questions

A GP-designed script checks for anything urgent

3

Right care, first time

You're directed to the best clinician or service for your need

4

Faster, safer care

Less waiting, quicker access to the help you need

Online Consultations
SMARTconsult

Submit a request to your GP online - no phone queues, no waiting rooms. Your request is reviewed by our clinical team and you'll receive a response, usually the same day.

  • Available anytime from your phone or computer
  • No need to call the surgery
  • Get advice, prescriptions, or referrals
  • Ideal for non-urgent medical concerns
1

Visit our website

Go to bramleyonline.co.uk and click "eConsultation"

2

Answer a few questions

Tell us about your symptoms or request

3

We review your request

A clinician reviews and responds, usually the same day

4

Receive your outcome

Advice, prescription, appointment, or onward referral
